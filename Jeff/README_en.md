# JEFF overdrive

The JEFF is a two-stage [JFET overdrive](https://github.com/akouz/MusEff/blob/main/Assymetrical/README_en.md#2-%D1%83%D1%81%D0%B8%D0%BB%D0%B8%D1%82%D0%B5%D0%BB%D1%8C%D0%BD%D1%8B%D0%B9-%D0%BA%D0%B0%D1%81%D0%BA%D0%B0%D0%B4-%D0%BD%D0%B0-jfet), similar to the single-stage [Polaris overdrive](https://github.com/akouz/MusEff/blob/main/Polaris/README_en.md). The Jeff was designed with two goals in mind:
- to compare the sound of a single- and dual-stage cascode asymmetrical JFET clipper
- to eliminate the TC1044, replacing it with a low-cost oscillator based on TC4S584 Schmitt trigger
