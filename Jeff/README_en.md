# JEFF overdrive

The JEFF is a two-stage [JFET overdrive](https://github.com/akouz/MusEff/blob/main/Assymetrical/README_en.md), similar to the single-stage [Polaris overdrive](https://github.com/akouz/MusEff/blob/main/Polaris/README_en.md). The Jeff was designed with two goals in mind:
- to compare the sound of a single- and dual-stage cascode asymmetrical JFET clipper
- to eliminate the TC1044, replacing it with a low-cost oscillator based on TC4S584 Schmitt trigger
