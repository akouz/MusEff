# Overdrive with asymmetrical diode clipper

An asymmetrical diode clipper was discussed [here](https://github.com/akouz/MusEff/blob/main/Assymetrical/README_en.md#1-biased-diode). The circuit shown in Fig. 1 uses a two-stage clipper. The first stage consists of resistor R6 and diodes D2 and D3. The second stage consists of resistor R9 and diode D4. 
Positive bias to the clipper is generated by diode D6 and adjusted by trimmer R16.
